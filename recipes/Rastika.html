Rastika

Something healthy from Hercegovina

Ingredients

  • 1 kg collard greens
  • 300 g prosciutto dry pork ribs or other smoked dry meat
  • 3-4 potatoes cubed
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic chopped
  • 1 bouillon cubes
  • 1-2 tsp paprika
  • salt pepper

Steps

  1. Cook dry meat in a pot of boiling water.
  2. Cut collard greens into larger ribbons, blanch in boiling water for about 3 minutes and drain
  3. In a large saucepan, sautee onion. Add drained collard greens, chopped garlic, ​​potatoes, salt, pepper, dried sliced ​​cooked meats and the bouillon cube. Add some water left over from cooking dry meat. Cook until collard / rastika is tender.
